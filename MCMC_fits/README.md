- Joint fit to Planck CMB, DESI BAO, Pantheon+ supernovae, SH0ES \(H_0\), and BBN abundances
- Best-fit parameters and \(\chi^2\) comparison with \(\Lambda\)CDM
- Resolution of \(H_0\) tension and lithium problem

Scripts and chain files included.
